The Villa

A home carved from light and stone

Tucked above a quiet cove, Villa Georgia Dream blends whitewashed Cycladic architecture with warm, contemporary interiors. Every room opens to the sea breeze, and every evening ends with the sky turning gold over the water. This is a house built for slow mornings, long lunches, and the kind of quiet that's hard to find anywhere else.

Sleeping up to eight guests across four bedrooms, the villa is ideal for families and friends seeking privacy, comfort and an authentic taste of island life.

  • 3 Bedrooms
  • 3 Bathrooms
  • 8 Guests
  • 209m² Living Space
